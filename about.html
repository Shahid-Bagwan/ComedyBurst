FIND THE BEST COMIC WHEREVER YOU ARE

ComdeyBurst has been a trusted home for specials since 2017. From day one, we’ve set about making it as easy, fun and fair as possible for you to see your favorite artists live. Right now, more than 60 million comic fans across the globe use ComedyBurst to track their favorite artists, discover events and buy tickets with joy.
We’ve always put fans first. That means no spam about things you don’t care about, and no inflated ticket prices. Live event should work for everyone, and we work hard to empower our community and cut out the BS along the way. Nothing but good vibes from here.
Best of all, ComedyBurst is totally free. Sign up and track comics via our website, or download our iPhone and Android apps for event alerts on the go. Either way, you’ll be the first to know about the best shows in your area. We’ll see you down the front.
